King Sargon of Akkad


Early life legend:

  • Mother was the high priestess of Azupiranu.
  • Placed in a sealed basket in a river as an infant.
  • Fell in love with Ishtar - the ancient Mesopotamian goddess of love, beauty, sex, war, justice and political power.
"In Agade [Akkad], Sargon, whose father was a gardener, the cupbearer of Ur-Zababa, became king, the king of Agade, who built Agade; he ruled for 56 years." - Sumerian Kings List
